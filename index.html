Decuple Chip Overload Cookies

10 kinds of chips. One legendary cookie.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar (packed)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chunks
  • 1/2 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup bittersweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup caramel-filled chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter chips
  • 1/2 cup mint chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup ruby chocolate chips

  • Cream together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  • Add eggs and vanilla extract; beat well.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt.
  • Gradually mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
  • Gently fold in all 10 types of chips until evenly distributed.
  • Scoop dough into balls and place on baking sheet with space between them.

  • Cool on baking sheet 5 minutes, then transfer to wire rack.
